Target Audience

The target audience for Weather on Point are both man and woman from 18 years old to 45 years old. These people will be active people that work or/and study during the week and do outdoor activities during the weekend and need a trustworthy weather website since they get out of their houses early in the morning and they come back later at night. They will surf their website through their phones as soon as they wake up in order to choose the proper outfit.

Two Personas

Jack Smith

He is a 41-year-old construction foreman. He works in several different construction projects, a part of his job is organizing the work so during the rainy days the contractors he leads can work on the project from the inside. He is also a family man that likes spending time with his children during the weekend. His son plays soccer, so He likes going to matches with him on Saturday nights.

Kate Pierce

Kate is a student from BYU. She likes hanging out with friends and family. She always says she feels trapped when she stays home for more than five hours. She loves outdoor and all sorts of extreme sports. She likes spontaneous trips to the lake and hiking the Y.
